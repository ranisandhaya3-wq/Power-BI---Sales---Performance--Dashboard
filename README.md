# Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard was developed to analyse sales performance, customer purchasing behaviour, and order cancellations. The dashboard provides interactive visualisations and key business metrics that help stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to:
1. Analyse overall sales performance.
2. Monitor revenue and order trends.
3. Track customer purchasing behaviour.
4. Measure the impact of cancelled orders on revenue.
5. Identify top-performing products and categories.
6. Create an interactive dashboard for business analysis.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks the following key business metrics:
 ### 1. Total Revenue
Definition: Total sales revenue generated during the selected period.
Purpose: Measures overall business performance.
### 2. Total Orders
Definition: Total number of customer orders received.
Purpose: Evaluates customer demand.
### 3. Average Order Value (AOV)
Formula:
AOV = Total Revenue ÷ Total Orders
Purpose: Measures the average amount spent by customers per order.
### 4. Cancellation Rate
Formula:
Cancellation Rate (%) = (Cancelled Orders ÷ Total Orders) × 100
Purpose: Measures the percentage of cancelled orders.
### 5. Lost Revenue Due to Cancellation
Definition: Total revenue lost because of cancelled orders.
Purpose: Quantifies the financial impact of order cancellations.
